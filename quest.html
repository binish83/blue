How do we encourage members to go and seek new experiences?

Quest for the best was a 5 day sprint as a UX student at Lambda School. My role as UX Designer/UX Researcher on the project made it a priority to work very closely with the stakeholder to build a usable and simple prototype. I researched, designed and tested all UI’s related to launch the project.

Houston! We have a problem.

So, What's the plan?

Well, It was crucial to assign the right amount of time to each tasks, so that I would be able to gain more time. With the clock ticking, I didn't have a team to implement a design sprint 2.0, and I didn't have the time to complete the entire product design cycle. So I decided to roll up my sleeves and do a hybrid version. Basically pick and choose the steps that would best solve the problem.
